About Us

Art Circle Foundation: Catalyzing a Cultural Revolution in Konkan

Art Circle Ratnagiri has become a transformative force in the Konkan region, bringing about a cultural renaissance through its dynamic initiatives and festivals. Since its inception, the organization has created a platform that bridges traditional Indian art forms with contemporary practices, making art and culture more accessible to the people of Ratnagiri and surrounding areas. As the organization has progressed, we have transformed into the Art Circle Foundation, a company where we can focus on cultural, academical, and environmental activities for the betterment of society.

Foundation History

Welcome to ART Circle Foundation

The ART Circle Foundation is a prominent organization dedicated to promoting art and craft culture. With over a decade of experience, the foundation has established itself as a leader in the field by organizing various events, competitions, and exhibitions to nurture and honor hidden artistic talents.

Vision of The ART Circle Foundation

To inspire and engage the community in the transformative power of art and culture, making it an integral part of everyday life, while contributing to the cultural, academic, and environmental betterment of society.

Mission of The ART Circle Foundation

To promote and preserve the rich cultural heritage of the Konkan region by bridging traditional Indian art forms with contemporary practices, fostering creativity, unity, and pride in local heritage through dynamic festivals, competitions, and initiatives.
